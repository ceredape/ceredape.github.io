About Me

The curiosity for understanding how electronics work took me to apply for an Electromechanical engineering career. In the second year we had some programming courses, I was amazed and hooked by them, so I changed paths. One year later with some good programming foundations my gamer me started to question if I could be a game developer. The course I was in wasn't remotelly close to fulfill that goal, and folliwing tutorials in Youtube felt like I wasn't getting nowhere near to understanding the whole process.

I left Argentina to study at ESAT, a well known and and recognized Spanish game development school.


Right after finishing college, I decided return to my country back with my family and friends where I joined Aconcagua Software, where my profesional career as a programmer started. After that I worked in Spellslingers and now in The Sandbox.

Currently I am looking forward to keep growing, in search of challenging projects and passionate people to work with.
